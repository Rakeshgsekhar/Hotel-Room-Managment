KOVALAM

Kovalam is the one of the famous seashore in  kerala.  It is famous for its wide range of tourism and travellings.  Kovalam means a grove of coconut trees and true to its name the village offers and endless site of coconut trees.Kovalam has three beaches sepoerated by rocky outcropping in its seventy kilometres coastline, the three together form the famous crescent of the kovalam beach.  

NAPIER MUSEUM

                                                                    The napier museum is an art and natural history museum situated in trivandrum.  The museum is about six kilometres from the city.  Open 10:00 AM to 04:45 PM.  closed on mondays, wednesday for noons, January 26, August 15, Thiruvonam and Mahanavami.
     the museum was established in 1855.  In 1874, the old museum building was demolished and foundation for the new building was laid.  The new building was named after Lord Napier, the Government of madras from 1866-72.

This temple is located in trivandrum.the temple is built in an intricate fusion of indeginious kerala style and the dravidian style.it is the richest hindu temple in the world.in terms of asset gold and pretious gold.
        the principal deity vishnu is enshrined in the "anantha shayanam".sree pathmanapha swamy is the tutelary deity of the royal family of travancore.

KUTHIRAMALIKA

                                                                  
Puthen malika palace most popularly known as kuthira malika(mansion of horses)is a place built by swathythirunal rama varma on the south eastern side of  pathmanabhaswami temple in trivandrum.
          The place gets the name from the 122 horses that are carved into the wooden wall brackets that support the southern roof.The offitial name of this palace is puthen malka(new mansion).The palace form  part of  a vast complex of royal building in the vicinity of pathmanabhaswamy temple.the building was left unoccupied for more than a century,following the  demise of swathy thirunal in 1846.

PAZHAVANGADI GANAPATHI TEMPLE

Pzhavangadi temple is situated in east fort in trivandrum.the main diety of this temple is sree mahaganapathy.the main idol is installed in a seated posture with the right leg in a folded stance.this  temple is situated in close proximity to the pathmanabhaswami temple,Godess Durga and Nagaraja.The temple sculptures include 32 different form of Lord Ganesha.
         The oroginal idol was maintain by the nair brigade initially at pathmanabhapuram and later when they were shifted to trivandrum they installed the idol and the current temple came into being.after the integration of the indian forces ,The temple is being maintained by indian army.

VIZHINJAM LIGHTHOUSE

Vizhinjam lighthouse is situated near kovalam beach in kerala.It started functioning on 30 june 1972.Vizhinjam was a busy seaport in the 18 th and 19 th centuries.Before the current light was installed, there were no light house at this location.a day mark beacon (Flag mast)must have been there in 18 th century.Afterr 19th century the port was a negleted state.A lighted beacon was constructed in 1925 at near by kolachal.subsequently a day mark beacon was provided at vizhinjam during 1960.
